Fretting Guitars is a guitar shop that sells both guitars and guitar parts, based in Austin, Texas. We are a family owned business, and also undertake repairs and offer DJ services. Once a week, on Fridays, we have live musicians come and play in our store. We aim to always be in tune with our customers' needs... we fret so you don't have to!
